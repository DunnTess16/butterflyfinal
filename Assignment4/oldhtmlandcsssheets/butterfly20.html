Variegated fritillary

Euptoieta claudia

Family:

Nymphalidae

Wing Span:

1 3/4 - 3 1/8 inches (4.5 - 8 cm).

Adult Food:

Nectar from several plant species including butterflyweed,

common milkweed, dogbane, peppermint, red clover, swamp milkweed, and tickseed sunflower.

Only when these are unavailable do Question Marks visit flowers such as common milkweed,

aster, and sweet pepperbush.

Flight:

Three broods from April-October in the north,

four broods from February or March-November or December in the south.
