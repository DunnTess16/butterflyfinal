American snout

Libytheana carinenta

Family:

Nymphalidae

Wing Span:

1 3/8 - 2 inches (3.5 - 5 cm).

Adult Food:

Nectar from flowers of aster, dogbane,

dogwood, goldenrod, sweet pepperbush, and others.

Flight:

Two broods; May-June, and August.

These butterflies sometimes undertake huge migrations.
