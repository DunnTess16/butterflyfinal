Viceroy

Limenitis archippus

Family:

Nymphalidaes

Wing Span:

2 1/2 - 3 3/8 inches (6.3 - 8.6 cm).

Adult Food:

Early in the season when few flowers are available Viceroys feed on aphid honeydew,

carrion, dung, and decaying fungi. Later generations feed more often at flowers,

favoring composites including aster, goldenrod, joe-pye weed, shepherd's needle, and Canada thistle.

Flight:

Two to three broods from May-September in most of its range, all year in Florida.
