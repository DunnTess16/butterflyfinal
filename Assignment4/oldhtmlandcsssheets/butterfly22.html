Lorquin's admiral

Limenitis lorquini

Family:

Nymphalidae

Wing Span:

2 - 2 5/8 inches (5.1 - 6.7 cm).

Adult Food:

Flower nectar from plants including California buckeye,

yerba santa, and privet; bird droppings; and dung.

Flight:

April-October.

Several flights in California, probably one flight in the northern part of its range.
