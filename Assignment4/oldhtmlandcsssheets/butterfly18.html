Question mark

Polygonia interrogationis

Family:

Nymphalidae

Wing Span:

2 1/4 - 3 inches (5.7 - 7.6 cm).

Adult Food:

Rotting fruit, tree sap, dung, carrion.

Only when these are unavailable do Question Marks visit flowers such as common milkweed,

aster, and sweet pepperbush.

Flight:

One or two broods from June-September.
