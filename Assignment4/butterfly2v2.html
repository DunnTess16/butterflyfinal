Gartia Skipper

Oarisma gartia

❮ ❯

Family:

Hesperiidae

Identification:

Upperside of wings ranges from orange to black. Underside of forewing is orange; hindwing is light brown with orange on the inner edge.

Habitat:

Short-grass prairie knolls, swales, limestone openings, open woodland, mountain meadows.

Flight:

One brood from June-July.

Adult Food:

Flower nectar.
