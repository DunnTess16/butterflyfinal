Rocky Mountain Parnassian

Parnassius smintheusa

❮ ❯

Family:

Papilionidae

Identification:

Antenna has alternate black and white rings. Upperside of forewing of females and most males with 2 red or yellow spots beyond the cell. In some males these spots are black.

Habitat:

Open forests, meadows, grasslands.

Flight:

TOne flight June-August.

Adult Food:

Nectar from flowers of Sedum and Asteraceae family.
